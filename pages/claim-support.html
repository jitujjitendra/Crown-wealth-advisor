Common Problems We Solve

Insurance Issues We Handle

❌ Claim Rejection

Health, life, or vehicle insurance claim rejected unfairly? We analyze the rejection reason and file proper appeals with supporting evidence.

⏰ Claim Delay

Waiting months for claim settlement? We escalate to higher authorities and ensure timely processing of your legitimate claims.

📄 Wrong Policy Sold

Purchased a policy different from what was promised? We help file mis-selling complaints and get policy corrections or refunds.

🚫 Policy Cancellation Issues

Unable to cancel or get refund on surrendered policy? We ensure proper cancellation processing and refund recovery.

💰 Premium Disputes

Premium increased unfairly or unauthorized deductions? We resolve billing disputes and ensure correct premium calculation.

📋 Documentation Problems

Claim documents rejected or need help preparing proper paperwork? We guide you step-by-step with correct documentation.

Our Process

How We Help You

  1. Case Assessment: Share your problem details and policy documents. Our experts analyze your case within 24 hours.
  2. Action Plan: We prepare a detailed action plan with required documents, escalation steps, and timeline.
  3. Grievance Filing: We draft and file formal complaints with insurance company grievance cell, nodal officer, or IRDAI.
  4. Follow-up & Escalation: Regular follow-up with insurance company. Escalate to Insurance Ombudsman if needed.
  5. Resolution: We work until your claim is settled or issue is resolved satisfactorily.

Success Rate: 85%+ cases resolved successfully. We only take cases where there is genuine merit and legal grounds for resolution.
