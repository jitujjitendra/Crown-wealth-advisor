Become a Insurance Advisor Partner

Join Crown Wealth Advisor as an insurance distribution partner. Explore growth-oriented opportunity with flexible working hours and comprehensive training support.

Why Partner With Us?

  • Attractive commission structure
  • Flexible working hours
  • Comprehensive product training
  • Ongoing marketing support
  • No upfront investment required

Partnership Benefits

What You Will Get

💰 Growth-Oriented Opportunity

Earn competitive commissions on every policy sold. Performance-based incentives and bonuses available for consistent performers.

📚 Complete Training

Get comprehensive training on leading insurance company products, sales techniques, and customer handling from experienced professionals.

🕐 Flexible Schedule

Work at your own pace with flexible timings. Perfect for those looking for part-time or full-time opportunities.

🎯 Marketing Support

Access to marketing materials, lead generation support, and digital tools to help you grow your business effectively.

🏆 Career Growth

Clear career progression path from advisor to senior positions. Recognition awards and performance incentives.

🤝 Trusted Brand

Represent leading insurance company, one of India's leading life insurance companies with a strong reputation and diverse product portfolio.

Requirements

Who Can Apply?

  • Age: Minimum 18 years
  • Education: Minimum 10th pass (12th preferred)
  • Communication: Good communication skills in Hindi/English and local language
  • Commitment: Willingness to learn and dedication to serve customers
  • Device: Smartphone with internet connection for digital processes
  • Background: No prior experience required - freshers welcome!

Note: Prior sales or insurance experience is an advantage but not mandatory. We provide complete training to help you succeed.

Application Process

How to Join?

Simple 4-Step Process:

  1. Apply: Fill the application form with your details
  2. Interview: Attend a brief telephonic/video interview
  3. Training: Complete product training and certification
  4. Start Earning: Begin your journey as an insurance partner

The entire process takes approximately 7-10 days. We'll guide you at every step.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is there any joining fee?

No, there is no upfront investment or joining fee required.

Q: Can I do this part-time?

Yes, you can work part-time or full-time based on your availability.

Q: What is the earning potential?

Earnings depend on your performance. Active partners typically earn ₹15,000 - ₹50,000+ per month.
