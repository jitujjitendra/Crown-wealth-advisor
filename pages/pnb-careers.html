Onboarding Journey

Your Path to Success

Complete 5-Step Onboarding:

  1. Apply: Submit your application through the form
  2. Screening: Initial interview and profile assessment
  3. Documentation: Submit KYC documents (Aadhaar, PAN, Photo)
  4. Training & Certification: Complete IRDAI exam preparation and obtain license
  5. Activation: Get your advisor code and start earning

Timeline: Complete onboarding in 15-20 days including IRDAI exam preparation.

Common Questions

Q: What is IRDAI license and how do I get it?

IRDAI (Insurance Regulatory Authority) license is mandatory. We provide free training and study material to help you pass the exam.

Q: Is there any investment required?

Minimal investment for IRDAI exam fee (approx. ₹500-800). No other upfront costs.

Q: How much can I earn monthly?

Active advisors earn ₹20,000 - ₹1,00,000+ per month depending on performance. Top performers earn significantly more.

Q: Can I work from home?

Yes! Work from anywhere with flexible hours. Meet clients at your convenience.
